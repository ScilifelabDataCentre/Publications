Labels are annotation tagged on to the publication references. They can be used to denote e.g. which facility or research group was involved in a publication, or what area the publication is in. This is set up by the site admin.

A curator account can have privileges for a set of labels. This means that she can add or remove those labels to a publication. She cannot change the labels for which she does not have privileges.

A curator can delete a publication. Obviously, this should be done respecting the wishes of other curators of the system.
